About the Clinic

Dermatology Rooted in
Trust & Clinical Excellence

Dr. Aditi's Skin and Laser Clinic is a dedicated dermatology practice in Sonipat, offering patient-centered care built on accurate diagnosis and honest treatment advice. Every consultation is focused on understanding your skin concern thoroughly before recommending the most appropriate — and often the most conservative — treatment path.

The clinic is designed to provide a calm, supportive environment where patients feel heard and respected. We believe in transparent communication, affordable care, and never recommending unnecessary procedures.

Accurate Diagnosis

Thorough clinical evaluation to identify the precise nature of your skin condition before any treatment begins.

Honest Treatment Advice

Conservative, appropriate recommendations — we never push unnecessary procedures or treatments.

Affordable Care

Quality dermatology care that remains accessible without compromising on clinical standards.

Calm Environment

A clean, well-maintained clinic space designed to make you feel comfortable and at ease.

Our Services

Comprehensive Dermatology

Clinical Dermatology

Acne Diagnosis & Treatment
